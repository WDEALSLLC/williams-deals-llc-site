About Us

Trusted online retailer serving Arizona and beyond since 2025.

Our Story

Williams Deals LLC is a family‑run online retailer proudly headquartered in Peoria, Arizona. According to the Better Business Bureau, the business opened its file on April 7, 2025 and began operations on April 19, 2025. We are organised as a Limited Liability Company and led by our founder and CEO, Kendall Williams. Over the past few years we've built a reputation for delivering unbeatable deals and friendly customer service across a wide range of products.

Although we began as a small ecommerce store, we now serve customers across Arizona and the wider United States. The BBB lists our categories as ecommerce, online retailer and general merchandise. Whether you're searching for mobility aids to help you stay on the move or power tools to tackle your next project, you can count on Williams Deals LLC for quality products at prices that fit your budget.

Our Mission & Values

  • Unbeatable Prices: We continuously monitor market prices and will match any competitor to ensure you always receive the best value.
  • Quality Products: We work with trusted brands like DeWalt, Milwaukee and Ridgid and only stock products we would use ourselves.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your experience matters. If you're not satisfied, we'll work to make it right with refunds, replacements or exchanges.
  • Community Focused: As an Arizona‑based business, we love giving back. We offer free local pickup and are always happy to answer your questions.
  • Easy Payments: We accept credit and debit cards, PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App and cash so you can pay however is most convenient for you.
